## General Questions

### What is T.A.S.K?
**Answer**: T.A.S.K (Telangana Academy for Skill and Knowledge) is a not-for-profit organization established by the Government of Telangana's ITE&C Department. It partners with academia and industry to skill youth and improve employability across Telangana state.

### Who can join T.A.S.K?
**Answer**: Engineering students (any year/branch), polytechnic students, graduates, unemployed youth, faculty members, and working professionals. Basic requirement: valid email and phone number.

### Is T.A.S.K government recognized?
**Answer**: Yes, T.A.S.K is established and operated by the Government of Telangana. It is DPDP 2025 compliant and has official policy support from the ITE&C department.

### Where is T.A.S.K located?
**Answer**: Head office: 1st Floor, Sanketika Vidya Bhavan, Masabtank, Hyderabad - 500028. T.A.S.K has 4 regional centers: Hyderabad (HQ), Khammam, Karimnagar, and Sircilla.

## Registration & Enrollment

### How do I register for T.A.S.K programs?
**Answer**: 
1. Visit https://www.task.telangana.gov.in
2. Click "Register" or "Student Login"
3. Create account with email and phone verification
4. Fill personal and academic information
5. Select programs (primary + 3 secondary interests)
6. Upload documents (College ID, Aadhar, Photo)
7. Complete payment (if applicable)
8. Receive confirmation email with Enrollment ID

### What documents are required for registration?
**Answer**: Valid college ID/identity, Aadhar card, proof of enrollment, email account, phone number. For graduates: degree certificate, 10th, Intermediate, and Graduation marksheets.

### How long does registration take?
**Answer**: 
- Immediate (24 hours): Portal access, LMS resources
- Within 48 hours: Orientation, assessment, peer groups
- Week 1: Program kickoff, industry partner introduction

### Can I register for multiple programs?
**Answer**: Yes, students can register for multiple programs with primary and secondary interests.

## Programs & Courses

### What programs does T.A.S.K offer?
**Answer**: T.A.S.K offers 50+ programs including AI & Machine Learning, Cloud Computing (AWS, Azure, GCP), Cybersecurity, Data Science, Full Stack Development, Java, Blockchain, IoT, VLSI, Manufacturing, Healthcare, and more.

### How much do programs cost?
**Answer**: Most programs are free or heavily subsidized (40-83% discounts). Some programs fully funded by government or industry partners. Financial assistance available for economically backward students.

### What is the duration of programs?
**Answer**: Program durations vary:
- Short programs: 40-60 hours (Java, Basic IT)
- Medium programs: 60-80 hours (Data Science, Full Stack)
- Long programs: 3-4 months (Cybersecurity, Finishing School)
- Intensive programs: 8-12 weeks (Finishing School)

### Are programs online or offline?
**Answer**: T.A.S.K offers both online and offline programs. Some programs are hybrid (combination of online and offline). Format depends on the specific program.

## Eligibility

### What is the minimum percentage required?
**Answer**: 
- Engineering students: 50% aggregate
- Polytechnic students: 50-60% aggregate
- Graduates: 60% in 10th, Intermediate, and Graduation

### Can 1st year students join?
**Answer**: Yes, T.A.S.K accepts engineering students from 1st year to 4th year, regardless of branch.

### Are all branches accepted?
**Answer**: Yes, T.A.S.K accepts students from all engineering branches (CS, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, Chemical, IT, etc.) and all polytechnic branches.

### What is the age limit?
**Answer**: For graduates and unemployed youth, preferably 18-35 years, though exceptions may be considered based on program requirements.

## Placement & Career

### What is the placement rate?
**Answer**: Overall: 85%+. Program-specific: ESDM 44%, Cybersecurity 78%, Finishing School 61%, General IT 88%+. Average salary ₹3.5-6.5 LPA depending on sector.

### How long does it take to get a job?
**Answer**: Typically 30-60 days after program completion. Internship to full-time conversion: 35-40%.

### Which companies hire from T.A.S.K?
**Answer**: Infosys, TCS, IBM, Microsoft, Google, Amazon, Verisk Analytics, Cognizant, Wipro, HCL, Mahindra, Safran, and 75+ other industry partners.

### What is the average salary?
**Answer**: 
- IT/Software: ₹3.5-6.5 LPA (Entry: ₹4.5 LPA)
- Cybersecurity: ₹4-5.5 LPA (Average: ₹4.5 LPA)
- Cloud Computing: ₹5.5-6.5 LPA
- Data Science: ₹4-5 LPA (Entry)
- Electronics/Manufacturing: ₹3-4 LPA (Entry)
- Healthcare: ₹2.5-3 LPA (Entry)
- Highest Package: ₹11.5 LPA (Verisk Analytics)

### Is placement guaranteed?
**Answer**: T.A.S.K provides placement support but does not guarantee placement. Placement depends on student performance, program completion, and industry requirements. Overall placement rate is 85%+.

## Financial Information

### Are programs free?
**Answer**: Most programs are free or heavily subsidized. AWS training is free, many programs have 40-83% discounts, and Finishing School is highly subsidized with government funding.

### What payment methods are accepted?
**Answer**: UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), NetBanking (all major banks), Debit Card, Credit Card, NEFT/RTGS transfer, Cheque (for organizations).

### Are scholarships available?
**Answer**: Yes, government scholarships for eligible students, economically backward student waivers, performance-based scholarships, and corporate sponsored programs are available.

### Can I get financial assistance?
**Answer**: Yes, financial assistance is available for economically backward students. Scholarships, waivers, and payment plans are available based on eligibility.

## Support & Services

### What support is provided?
**Answer**: Training, certification, internship placement, job placement, mentor assignment, career counseling, industry exposure, and optional financial assistance.

### Is there career counseling available?
**Answer**: Yes, T.A.S.K provides career counseling, resume building assistance, interview preparation, and placement support.

### Are mentors assigned?
**Answer**: Yes, mentorship programs are available through partners like Mentor Together, with 1-on-1 mentorship, 600+ mentee-mentor pairs, and 6-month mentorship cycles.

### What is the Finishing School program?
**Answer**: Finishing School is an 8-12 week intensive program for graduates and unemployed youth, focusing on technical skills and soft skills development, with 61% placement rate and average salary ₹2.8-4.2 LPA.

## Technical Support

### How do I access the student portal?
**Answer**: Visit https://www.task.telangana.gov.in/login and use your registered email and password.

### What if I forget my password?
**Answer**: Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page. Password reset link will be sent to your registered email.

### How do I update my profile?
**Answer**: Log in to the student portal and navigate to "My Profile" section to update personal and academic information.

### Who do I contact for technical issues?
**Answer**: Email enquiry_task@telangana.gov.in or call +91-40-35485290 during office hours (Monday-Friday, 9:30 AM - 5:00 PM IST).

## Compliance & Privacy

### Is T.A.S.K DPDP compliant?
**Answer**: Yes, T.A.S.K is fully compliant with DPDP Act 2025. Data retention is 7 years (regulatory requirement), no third-party sharing without consent, annual privacy audits, and user consent required for all data usage.

### How is my data protected?
**Answer**: SSL/TLS encryption, PII masking in logs, regular security audits, DDoS protection, 3-region backup redundancy, and 99.9% Uptime SLA.
