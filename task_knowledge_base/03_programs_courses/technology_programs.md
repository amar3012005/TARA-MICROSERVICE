## Common Questions Answered

**Q: Which technology programs are free?**  
A: AWS training is free (AWS Educate Program), Java Programming is free (Oracle Academy), and Google Cloud has free tier available.

**Q: What is the most popular technology program?**  
A: Cloud Computing (AWS) has reached 2,000+ students, followed by Data Science (2,051+ students) and Cybersecurity (500+ students).

**Q: What is the highest paying technology program?**  
A: Cloud Computing programs (AWS, Azure, GCP) offer average salaries of ₹5.5-6.5 LPA, followed by AI & ML at ₹5-6 LPA.

**Q: Do technology programs guarantee placement?**  
A: T.A.S.K provides placement support but does not guarantee placement. However, programs like Cybersecurity guarantee internship placements, and overall placement rates range from 78-88%.

**Q: Can I do multiple technology programs?**  
A: Yes, students can register for multiple programs with primary and secondary interests. Many students combine programs like Cloud Computing with Data Science or Cybersecurity.
