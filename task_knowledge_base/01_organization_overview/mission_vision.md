## Mission Statement

**Primary Mission**: Skilling youth and making them more employable while curbing unemployment in Telangana State

### Mission Breakdown
- **Target**: Youth of Telangana
- **Objective**: Skill development and employability enhancement
- **Impact Goal**: Reduce unemployment through quality training
- **Geographic Focus**: Telangana State (all 33 districts)

---

## Vision Statement

**Primary Vision**: Transform Telangana into a hub for technological advancements through skill development

### Vision Components
- **Geographic Ambition**: Make Telangana a technology hub
- **Method**: Skill development programs
- **Outcome**: Technological advancement and innovation
- **Long-term Impact**: State-wide transformation
