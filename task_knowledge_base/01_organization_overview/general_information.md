## Organization Identity

### Full Name

### Official Designation
- **Type**: Not-for-Profit Organization (NGO)
- **Established By**: Government of Telangana (2014)

## Common Questions Answered

**Q: What is T.A.S.K?**  
A: T.A.S.K (Telangana Academy for Skill and Knowledge) is a not-for-profit organization established by the Government of Telangana's ITE&C Department. It partners with academia and industry to skill youth and improve employability across Telangana state.

**Q: Is T.A.S.K government recognized?**  
A: Yes, T.A.S.K is established and operated by the Government of Telangana. It is DPDP 2025 compliant and has official policy support from the ITE&C department.

**Q: Where is T.A.S.K located?**  
A: Head office is in Hyderabad at 1st Floor, Sanketika Vidya Bhavan, Masabtank, Hyderabad - 500028. T.A.S.K has 4 regional centers: Hyderabad (HQ), Khammam, Karimnagar, and Sircilla.

**Q: How many students has T.A.S.K trained?**  
A: T.A.S.K has trained 50,000+ students over 5 years, with 5,000+ students trained annually. Currently, 10,000+ students are registered.
