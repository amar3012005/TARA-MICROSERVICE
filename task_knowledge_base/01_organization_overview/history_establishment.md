**Document Type**: Historical Background  
**Last Updated**: December 2025

## Establishment

### Founding
- **Year**: 2014
- **Established By**: Government of Telangana
- **Parent Ministry**: ITE&C Department (Information Technology, Electronics & Communications)
- **Legal Status**: Not-for-Profit Organization (NGO)
- **Registration**: Government Organization

### Context
T.A.S.K was established as part of the Government of Telangana's initiative to address youth unemployment and skill gaps in the state. The organization was created to bridge the gap between academic education and industry requirements.
