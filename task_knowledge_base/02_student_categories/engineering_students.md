## Eligibility Requirements

### Basic Requirements
- **Status**: Currently enrolled in engineering program
- **Minimum Aggregate**: 50%
- **Year Eligibility**: 1st year to 4th year
- **Branch Eligibility**: All branches accepted

### Accepted Branches
- **Computer Science (CS)**
- **Electronics & Communications (ECE)**
- **Electrical & Electronics (EEE)**
- **Mechanical Engineering**
- **Civil Engineering**
- **Chemical Engineering**
- **Information Technology (IT)**
- **All other engineering branches**

### Documentation Required
- **College ID**: Valid college identification
- **Email**: Active email account
- **Phone Number**: Valid mobile number
- **Academic Records**: Proof of enrollment and marks

## Registration Process

### Step 1: Portal Access
- Visit: https://www.task.telangana.gov.in
- Click "Register" or "Student Login"

### Step 2: Account Creation
- Email verification
- Password setup
- Phone number verification

### Step 3: Academic Information
- College name
- Engineering branch
- Current year (1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th)
- Aggregate percentage

### Step 4: Program Selection
- Primary program interest
- 3 secondary interests
- Preferred batch timing

### Step 5: Document Upload
- College ID
- Academic transcripts
- Photo

### Step 6: Payment (if applicable)
- Most programs: Free or subsidized
- Payment methods: UPI, NetBanking, Cards

### Step 7: Confirmation
- Email confirmation
- Enrollment ID
- Program start date

## Common Questions Answered

**Q: Can 1st year engineering students join?**  
A: Yes, T.A.S.K accepts engineering students from 1st year to 4th year, regardless of branch.

**Q: What is the minimum percentage required?**  
A: Engineering students need a minimum aggregate of 50% to be eligible for T.A.S.K programs.

**Q: Are all engineering branches accepted?**  
A: Yes, T.A.S.K accepts students from all engineering branches including CS, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, Chemical, IT, and others.

**Q: How much do programs cost for engineering students?**  
A: Most programs are free or heavily subsidized. For example, AI & ML costs ₹2,500 (83% discount), AWS is free, and many other programs have 40-70% discounts.

**Q: What is the placement rate for engineering students?**  
A: Overall placement rate is 85%+, with IT sector achieving 88%+ placement rate. Average salary ranges from ₹3.5-6.5 LPA.
