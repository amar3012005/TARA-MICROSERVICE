## Eligibility Requirements

### Basic Requirements
- **Education**: Bachelor's degree holder

- **Age Range**: Preferably 18-35 years

  - **Intermediate**: ≥60%
  - **Graduation**: ≥60%

### Documentation Required
- **Degree Certificate**: Bachelor's degree certificate
- **10th Marksheet**: Minimum 60%
- **Intermediate Marksheet**: Minimum 60%
- **Graduation Marksheet**: Minimum 60%
- **Email**: Active email account
- **Phone Number**: Valid mobile number

## Available Programs

### Finishing School Program

#### Program Overview
- **Target**: Graduates and unemployed youth
- **Duration**: 8-12 weeks
- **Format**: Intensive training program
- **Cost**: Highly subsidized, government funded
- **Focus**: Technical skills + Soft skills finishing school

#### Program Details
- **Total Enrolled**: 3,677
- **Successfully Placed**: 2,230
- **Placement Percentage**: 61%
- **Average Salary**: ₹2.8-4.2 LPA
- **Time to Placement**: 45-60 days

#### Curriculum Components
- **Technical Skills**: Industry-relevant technical training
- **Soft Skills**: Communication, teamwork, problem-solving
- **Interview Preparation**: Mock interviews, resume building
- **Industry Exposure**: Guest lectures, industry visits
- **Placement Support**: Dedicated placement assistance

### Sector-Specific Programs

#### IT & Software
- **Focus**: Programming, web development, software testing
- **Duration**: 8-12 weeks
- **Placement Rate**: 88%+
- **Average Salary**: ₹3.5-5 LPA

#### Data Science & Analytics
- **Partner**: IBM SmartBridge
- **Duration**: 60 hours
- **Certifications**: IBM Data Science, IBM AI Engineering
- **Placement Support**: Yes

#### Cloud Computing
- **AWS**: Free (AWS Educate Program)
- **Microsoft Azure**: Subsidized (50-70% discount)
- **Google Cloud**: Free tier available

#### Cybersecurity Training
- **Partner**: Cotelligent
- **Duration**: 3-4 months
- **Placement Rate**: 78%
- **Average Salary**: ₹4.2-5.5 LPA
- **Guaranteed**: Internship placements

#### Healthcare Reskilling Program
- **Partner**: National Health Mission (NHM), Telangana Vaidya Vidhan Parishad (TVVP)
- **Target**: Healthcare workers, nursing staff, paramedics
- **Healthcare Workers Trained**: 1,826+
- **Duration**: 4-8 weeks
- **Placement Rate**: 55%
- **Content**: Advanced nursing, Patient care, Health & safety, Medical technology

#### Manufacturing Programs
- **Partner**: Hindustan Coca-Cola Beverages (HCCB)
- **Target**: 10,000 unemployed youth
- **Duration**: 8-week intensive program
- **Placement Rate**: 52%
- **Salary Range**: ₹2.5-3.5 LPA
- **Content**: Manufacturing, supply chain, quality training

## Registration Process

### Step 1: Portal Access
- Visit: https://www.task.telangana.gov.in
- Click "Register" or "Student Login"

### Step 2: Account Creation
- Email verification
- Password setup
- Phone number verification

### Step 3: Personal Information
- Name, DOB, Gender, Address
- Employment status (unemployed)
- Current location

### Step 4: Academic Information
- 10th marks (≥60% required)
- Intermediate marks (≥60% required)
- Graduation details (≥60% required)
- Degree certificate upload

### Step 5: Program Selection
- Primary program interest (Finishing School recommended)
- 3 secondary interests
- Preferred batch timing

### Step 6: Document Upload
- Degree certificate
- 10th marksheet
- Intermediate marksheet
- Graduation marksheet
- Aadhar card
- Photo

### Step 7: Payment (if applicable)
- Finishing School: Highly subsidized
- Other programs: Free or subsidized
- Payment methods: UPI, NetBanking, Cards

### Step 8: Confirmation
- Email confirmation
- Enrollment ID
- Program start date
- Orientation details

## Placement Support

### Placement Statistics
- **Finishing School**: 61% placement rate (2,230 placed out of 3,677)
- **Average Salary**: ₹2.8-4.2 LPA
- **Time to Placement**: 45-60 days after program completion
- **Top Sectors**: IT, Manufacturing, Healthcare, Data Science

### Placement Process
1. **Training Completion**: Finish program successfully
2. **Assessment**: Complete assessments and certifications
3. **Profile Creation**: Build professional profile
4. **Interview Preparation**: Mock interviews and resume help
5. **Placement Drive**: Participate in placement drives
6. **Job Offer**: Receive and accept job offer
7. **Post-Placement Support**: Follow-up and support

## Common Questions Answered

**Q: Can unemployed graduates join T.A.S.K programs?**  
A: Yes, T.A.S.K specifically targets graduates and unemployed youth. The Finishing School program is designed for this category.

**Q: What is the minimum percentage required?**  
A: Minimum 60% in 10th, Intermediate, and Graduation is required for most programs.

**Q: What is the age limit?**  
A: Preferably 18-35 years, though exceptions may be considered based on program requirements.

**Q: What is the Finishing School program?**  
A: Finishing School is an 8-12 week intensive program designed for graduates and unemployed youth, focusing on technical skills and soft skills development, with 61% placement rate.

**Q: How much does Finishing School cost?**  
A: Finishing School is highly subsidized and government-funded. Most students pay minimal or no fees.

**Q: What is the placement rate for graduates?**  
A: Finishing School has 61% placement rate (2,230 placed out of 3,677). Average salary ranges from ₹2.8-4.2 LPA, with placement typically occurring within 45-60 days.

**Q: Are there programs for specific sectors?**  
A: Yes, T.A.S.K offers sector-specific programs including IT, Data Science, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, Healthcare, and Manufacturing.
