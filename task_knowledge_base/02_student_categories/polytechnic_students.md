## Eligibility Requirements

### Basic Requirements
- **Status**: Currently enrolled in polytechnic/diploma program OR diploma holder
- **Minimum Aggregate**: 50-60% (varies by program)
- **Branch Eligibility**: All branches accepted
- **Year Eligibility**: All years accepted

### Accepted Branches
- **Electronics**
- **Mechanical**
- **Civil**
- **Information Technology (IT)**
- **Manufacturing**
- **Electrical**
- **Automobile**
- **All other polytechnic branches**

### Documentation Required
- **Diploma Certificate**: Valid diploma or enrollment proof
- **College ID**: Valid college identification
- **Email**: Active email account
- **Phone Number**: Valid mobile number
- **Academic Records**: Proof of marks and enrollment

## Available Programs

### Technical Skill Development Programs

#### ESDM Scheme (Electronics & Semiconductor Manufacturing)
- **Partner**: Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology (MeitY)
- **Total Trained**: 5,370+
- **Successfully Placed**: 2,360+
- **Placement Percentage**: 44%
- **Average Salary**: ₹3.5-4.5 LPA
- **Skills**: Circuit board assembly, Soldering, Component testing, Quality inspection, Manufacturing operations

#### Manufacturing & Quality Programs
- **Partner**: Mahindra Pride Classroom, Safran Electrical & Power
- **Target**: Final-year polytechnic students
- **Content**: Manufacturing processes, Quality operations, QA inspection
- **Reach**: 1,200+ students annually
- **Placement Support**: Direct hiring pathways

#### Cybersecurity Training
- **Partner**: Cotelligent
- **Duration**: 3-4 months
- **Placement Rate**: 78%
- **Average Salary**: ₹4.2-5.5 LPA
- **Guaranteed**: Internship placements

#### Cloud Computing - AWS
- **Partner**: Amazon Web Services
- **Cost**: Free (AWS Educate Program)
- **Certifications**: AWS Certified Solutions Architect, AWS Certified Developer

#### Cisco IT & Security
- **Partner**: Cisco
- **Courses**: IT Essentials, CCNA, CCENT, Security Essentials, IoT
- **Duration**: 150-210 hours per course
- **Reach**: 1,500+ students
- **Cost**: Subsidized for colleges

#### Java Programming
- **Partner**: Oracle Academy
- **Duration**: 40-60 hours
- **Cost**: Free
- **Certification**: Oracle Certified Associate Java Programmer

#### Full Stack Development
- **Duration**: 80 hours
- **Level**: Intermediate
- **Cost**: Subsidized

## Specialized Programs

### Finishing School
- **Target**: Diploma holders seeking employment
- **Duration**: 8-12 weeks
- **Focus**: Technical skills + Soft skills finishing school
- **Cost**: Highly subsidized, government funded
- **Placement Support**: Yes

### Sector-Specific Training

#### Electronics & Manufacturing
- **Focus**: Circuit design, assembly, testing
- **Placement**: Manufacturing companies
- **Salary Range**: ₹3-4 LPA

#### IT & Software
- **Focus**: Programming, web development, software testing
- **Placement**: IT companies
- **Salary Range**: ₹3.5-5 LPA

#### Quality Assurance
- **Focus**: QA inspection, quality control
- **Placement**: Manufacturing and IT sectors
- **Salary Range**: ₹3-4 LPA

## Registration Process

### Step 1: Portal Access
- Visit: https://www.task.telangana.gov.in
- Click "Register" or "Student Login"

### Step 2: Account Creation
- Email verification
- Password setup
- Phone number verification

### Step 3: Academic Information
- Polytechnic/College name
- Diploma branch
- Current year or completion status
- Aggregate percentage

### Step 4: Program Selection
- Primary program interest
- 3 secondary interests
- Preferred batch timing

### Step 5: Document Upload
- Diploma certificate or enrollment proof
- College ID
- Academic transcripts
- Photo

### Step 6: Payment (if applicable)
- Most programs: Free or subsidized
- Payment methods: UPI, NetBanking, Cards

### Step 7: Confirmation
- Email confirmation
- Enrollment ID
- Program start date

## Placement Support

### Placement Statistics
- **ESDM Scheme**: 44% placement rate (2,360+ placed)
- **Manufacturing Programs**: Direct hiring pathways
- **Average Salary**: ₹3-4.5 LPA
- **Top Sectors**: Manufacturing, IT, Electronics, Quality Assurance

### Placement Process
1. **Training Completion**: Finish program successfully
2. **Certification**: Obtain industry certification
3. **Profile Creation**: Build professional profile
4. **Interview Preparation**: Mock interviews and resume help
5. **Placement Drive**: Participate in placement drives
6. **Job Offer**: Receive and accept job offer

### Top Recruiting Companies
- **Manufacturing**: Mahindra & Mahindra, Safran, Automotive companies
- **IT**: Infosys, TCS, HCL, Wipro
- **Electronics**: Electronics manufacturing companies
- **Quality**: QA and inspection companies

## Common Questions Answered

**Q: Can polytechnic students join T.A.S.K programs?**  
A: Yes, T.A.S.K accepts polytechnic students from all branches and all years, including diploma holders.

**Q: What is the minimum percentage required for polytechnic students?**  
A: Minimum aggregate of 50-60% is required, depending on the specific program.

**Q: Are all polytechnic branches accepted?**  
A: Yes, T.A.S.K accepts students from all polytechnic branches including Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, IT, Manufacturing, Electrical, and others.

**Q: What programs are available for polytechnic students?**  
A: Programs include ESDM Scheme, Manufacturing & Quality, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, Cisco IT, Java Programming, and Finishing School programs.

**Q: What is the placement rate for polytechnic students?**  
A: ESDM Scheme has 44% placement rate (2,360+ placed). Manufacturing programs have direct hiring pathways. Average salary ranges from ₹3-4.5 LPA.

**Q: Are programs free for polytechnic students?**  
A: Most programs are free or heavily subsidized. ESDM Scheme and Finishing School are government-funded. Other programs have 40-70% discounts.
