## Common Questions Answered

**Q: What is T.A.S.K's overall placement rate?**  
A: T.A.S.K has an overall placement rate of 85%+, with program-specific rates ranging from 44% (ESDM) to 88%+ (IT sector).

**Q: How long does it take to get a job after program completion?**  
A: Typically 30-60 days after program completion. Cybersecurity programs see placement in 20-30 days, while Finishing School takes 45-60 days.

**Q: What is the average salary?**  
A: Average salary ranges from ₹2.5-6.5 LPA depending on sector. IT/Software: ₹3.5-6.5 LPA, Cloud Computing: ₹5.5-6.5 LPA, Cybersecurity: ₹4-5.5 LPA.

**Q: Which companies hire from T.A.S.K?**  
A: Top companies include Infosys, TCS, IBM, Microsoft, Google, Amazon, Verisk Analytics, Cognizant, Wipro, HCL, Mahindra, Safran, and 75+ other industry partners.

**Q: Is placement guaranteed?**  
A: T.A.S.K provides comprehensive placement support but does not guarantee placement. Placement depends on student performance, program completion, and industry requirements. However, overall placement rate is 85%+.

**Q: What is the highest salary package?**  
A: Highest package recorded is ₹11.5 LPA from Verisk Analytics. Average packages range from ₹3.5-6.5 LPA depending on sector and program.
